Who we are

Kappa Delta Chi Sorority, Inc. is a Latina founded national sorority who aims to achieve professional development, academic excellence, and graduation of all its members; an organization dedicated to community service to their local university communities with an emphasis on the Hispanic/Latino population.

What we do

Academics

Our main goal for attending college is to graduate. KDChi helps facilitate this with other like-minded individuals through academic workshops held throughout the school year.

Service

We love to do community service all around the Lansing area. It is in our nature to give back and with sisters that feel the same way means that much bigger of an impact.

Professionalism

We don't just stop after graduation. We network with other professionals, collaborate with other Greek Organizations, and host resume workshops in the Fall and Spring.

What we value

Unity, Honesty, Integrity, and Leadership. These are the four values we abide by in our daily lives. When we wear these letters and throw our "K", we are proud to represent Kappa Delta Chi Sorority, Inc. and its growing legacy. We are always "Leading with Integrity and United through Service".
